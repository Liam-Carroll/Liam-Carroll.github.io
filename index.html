Hi,

My name is Liam Carroll, but I am sure you could have guessed that thanks to the huge print at the top of the page.
It would be kind of weird if that was someone else's name
I am currently working towards completing my third year as a computer science student at Stonehill College. My interest in computer science was piqued when I was very young. After accidentally opening developer tools and poking around I was fascinated by all the underlying components that made up everything I saw and interacted with on the web. These words and symbols made up a language I would eventually come to know as HTML. A few discrete math classes later and I am no longer that niave child staring in wonder at my computer screen but I can't help thinking my computer science career has come full circle, in a way, as I sit here looking at those same works and symbols, designing my very own personal website.
