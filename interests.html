What I do for fun

Photography

I recently purchased a Nikon d3500. When I have time one of my favorite things to do is go out exploring with my camera and some friends. I'm currently working on setting up a Flikr or VSCO which I'll link below.

Digital Compositing

Making unique designs, landscapes, even portraits through extensively layering and editing different sources together is one of those hobbies that I can just lose myself in. I may sit down with no clear intention in mind but hours still fly by, every step of the way I produce something I might save and consider complete, only to apply a different technique to it and take the piece in an entirely different direction.

Drawing

I often find myself getting a little too attatched to the doodles I make in the margins. Yet another skill I have limitless room to improve upon is old-fashioned pencil-to-paper drawing.
